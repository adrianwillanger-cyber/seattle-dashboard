Market Intelligence
🏡
98115 — Premium but Softening
Wedgwood / Ravenna commands the highest prices at $940K median, down 6% YoY. Price/sqft remains highest at $566. DOM at 26 days signals lasting demand from quality-conscious buyers. School proximity and quiet interior streets sustain the premium.
📉
98125 — Biggest Correction, Early Recovery
Lake City / Meadowbrook saw the steepest YoY drop at −10.7%, bringing median to $790K. Despite a +22% inventory surge, homes are now selling faster than last year (DOM −10 days), hinting the correction may be nearing a floor. ADU-capable lots add long-term upside.
🔥
98155 — Most Competitive, Mildest Drop
Shoreline/Northgate has the highest Compete Score at 89/100, averages 4 offers per listing, and has the smallest YoY price decline at −3.7%. Tightest inventory of the three. January 2026 saw 68 homes sold, $825K median, 98.2% list-to-sale ratio.
📊
vs. City Average
Seattle citywide median sits at $849,500 (−0.35% YoY). All three ZIPs outperform city pricing but are correcting faster — suggesting these north neighborhoods led the 2024 cycle peak. ADU/DADU flexibility and HB 1110 density rules add longer-term land value support.
